We will train you

One might have a different style of playing cricket but to nurture and improvise this talent, necessary guidance from experienced players is important to shine at all fronts. This base is provided by joining some top cricket academies where the focus is given to all age groups under a competitive environment to learn the most basic as well as advanced tactics of cricket.The game itself brings out deep emotions amongst Indians which vary from happiness to sadness, excitement, and pride to crying. Cricket is an emotion that Indians have felt and loved every bit of it.

JS is the popular cricket academy in Bangalore, founded by an outstanding member of India’s cricket team, Mr. Jagan Satwik. The academy is a host to many international players, coaches holding regular practice matches with hostel facilities for visiting players. JS holds a summer camp for all age groups from 6 and above post-summer examinations with 4 practice sessions in a week. Floodlights are provided for all practice sessions after 7 PM.
